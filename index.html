Sharecoin

$share is a digital currency that operates on smart-contracts and follow the principles of the decentralization. Anyone can get $share as reward for inviting other people into decentralized finances.


To get $share you need

  1. Send 1 $ton to someone's invite-contractAn invite-contract is a self-executing smart-contract with the terms of the agreement between buyer and seller directly written into lines of code.
    Once you do so, you'll receive your first $shares as a reward.
    Also it creates your own invite-contract
  2. Share your invite-contract with others
  3. Get more $share for every invited user

Just send it to the ✰ treasury-contractThe treasury-contract is a smart-contract that acts as a repository for $ton in the system.

You will receive 1,5 $ton for 1 $share:
